# Personal AI Vault - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Vision

Personal AI Vault is an AI-powered identity platform that transforms documents into structured knowledge.

Instead of simply storing files, the platform understands people, relationships, documents, credentials, addresses, education, employment history, and other identity-related information.

The system continuously builds a Personal Identity Graph that can be searched, reused, verified, and applied to automate tasks such as form filling and document retrieval.

Vision Statement:

"Store information once. Understand it forever. Reuse it everywhere."

---

## Problem

Users repeatedly:

* Fill the same forms.
* Search for the same documents.
* Upload the same proofs.
* Manage multiple identity documents.
* Maintain family records.
* Verify information across services.

Current solutions focus on either:

* Storage
* OCR
* Password Management
* Autofill

None create a reusable understanding of the user's identity.

---

### Primary Goals

1. Create a structured identity graph from documents.

1. Smart document sharing.

* Automatic categorization

* Confidence Score
